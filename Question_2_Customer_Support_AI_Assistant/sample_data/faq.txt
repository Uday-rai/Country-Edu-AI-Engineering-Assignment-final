Company Support FAQ

Refunds:
Customers can request a refund within 7 days of purchase if the product has not been used heavily and the order is eligible under company policy.

Shipping:
Standard shipping normally takes 3 to 5 business days. Express shipping normally takes 1 to 2 business days.

Password Reset:
Customers can reset their password by selecting Forgot Password on the login page. A secure reset link will be sent to the registered email address.

Support Hours:
Human support agents are available from 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Saturday.

Escalation:
Billing disputes, legal complaints, account ownership conflicts, and technical outages should be escalated to a human support specialist.
